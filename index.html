Our Doctor

Christine Yuanbing Liao

Dr. Christine Y. Liao received her Bachelor of Medicine degree (equivalent to Doctor of Medicine in the USA) with honors from the prestigious Sun Yat-sen University, School of Medicine, in Guangzhou, China. She practiced as an ophthalmologist for almost ten years in Zhongshan Ophthalmic Center of Sun Yat-sen University after graduation.

Dr. Liao went on to receive her Doctor of Optometry from The New England College of Optometry in Boston, Massachusetts. She was the recipient of William R. Baldwin Scholastic Achievement Award, which honors the highest cumulative GPA excellence.

Dr. Liao is a California TLG-certified licensed optometrist. She can use therapeutic pharmaceutical agents for different eye diseases, perform lacrimal irrigation, dilation and treat glaucoma. She is also a Paragon CRT-certified optometrist and is able to perform corneal refractive therapy with Paragon CRT contact lens to control myopia. Dr. Liao has practiced in San Francisco Bay Area since her graduation.
